Biography

Martin Goodfellow received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering from ETH Zurich in 2012 and 2015 respectively. Since 2015, he is a PhD student at the Computer Engineering Department under the supervision of Prof. Mike Johnson. His research focus is the modeling Convolutional Neural Networks.
